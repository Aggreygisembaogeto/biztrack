## 🧠 AI Intelligence Features

### 1. Smart Inventory Alerts
The AI analyzes your inventory against current market prices:

**Example**:
```
🟡 Cooking Oil Stock Alert
Cooking oil is low (5 liters). Market price is increasing (+12%). 
Consider waiting if prices stabilize.
```

**How it works**:
- Monitors your inventory levels
- Compares with Kenya market prices
- Recommends best time to restock
- Alerts on price trends (up/down/stable)

### 2. Sales Performance Analysis
Compares your sales against targets and market trends:

**Example**:
```

Today's revenue (KES 6,500) is 30% above target! Keep up the momentum.
```

**Metrics**:
- Daily revenue vs target
- Weekly performance trends
- Comparison with market averages
- Growth recommendations

for popular items.

**Time-based alerts**:
- 11:00-14:00: Lunch rush warning

- Staffing recommendations
- Inventory preparation tips

### 4. Economic Impact Analysis
Monitors Kenya economic indicators affecting your business:

maintain margins.

**Tracks**:
