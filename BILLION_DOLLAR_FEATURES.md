## ✨ New Premium Features

### 1. 🚨 Alerts Center (Real-time Intelligence)

**Enterprise-grade alert system** that monitors your business 24/7 and provides actionable insights.

#### Alert Types:

**Critical Alerts** (🔴 Red):
- Out of stock items
- Critical stock levels (below 50% of minimum)
- Negative profit (expenses > sales)
- Operating at loss

**Warning Alerts** (🟡 Yellow):
- Low stock items (at minimum threshold)
- Low profit margin (<20%)
- High expense ratio (>70% of sales)
- Aging inventory (30+ days old)

**Info Alerts** (🔵 Blue):
- Low sales activity
- Pending orders (>5)
- Inventory insights

**Success Alerts** (🟢 Green):
- Excellent performance
- High profit margins (>40%)
- Strong sales day

#### Features:
- ✅ Real-time monitoring
- ✅ Smart categorization
- ✅ Actionable recommendations
- ✅ Filter by alert type
- ✅ Auto-refresh capability
- ✅ Timestamp tracking
- ✅ Priority sorting

**Access**: Click "Alerts" button on Dashboard or Inventory page
