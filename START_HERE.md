## 🎯 For New Users

### Step 1: Open the App
Go to: **http://localhost:3000**

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Click **"Create Account"**
2. Enter your details:
   - Email
   - Password
   - Business Name
   - Phone (optional)
   - Address (optional)
3. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 3: Start Using BizTrack!
You're now logged in and can:
- ✅ Track sales and inventory
- ✅ Manage orders from multiple platforms
- ✅ Monitor finances
- ✅ Generate reports
- ✅ And much more!

📚 **Need help?** Read **GETTING_STARTED.md** for complete instructions.
